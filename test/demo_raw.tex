\subsection* {Raw embedded applications are sticky}

The raw embedded application is not automatically killed or unmap by
{\ActiveDVI}. Hence you have to do it yourself!

\advirecord{unmap}
 {\adviunmapembed[advilogo]}

\advirecord{map}
 {\advimapembed[advilogo]}

\begin{itemize}
\item \advianchor[click]{map}
  {Map the {\ActiveDVI} logo window}
\item \advianchor[click]{unmap}
  {Unmap the {\ActiveDVI} logo window}
\end{itemize}

\adviwait \advikillallembed{advianim}

\subsection* {Fake embedded applications are sticky}

\advirecord{createfake}{\centerline{Here is $\quad\quad${\adviembed[fake=fakelogo,width=3cm, height=3.3260869cm]{ls}}} the fake window}

Thanks to the {\tt fake} embedded application, space is allocated for
the raw application.

\advirecord{unmap}{\adviunmapembed[advilogo]}

\advirecord{map}{\advimapembed[advilogo]}

\advirecord{unmapfake}{\adviunmapembed[fakelogo]}

\advirecord{mapfake}{\advimapembed[fakelogo]}

\advirecord{closefake}{\advikillembed{fakelogo}}

Now choose one of the following:

\begin{itemize}
\item \advianchor[click]{createfake}
  {Create the {\ActiveDVI} logo fake window}
\item \advianchor[click]{mapfake}
  {Map the {\ActiveDVI} logo fake window}
\item \advianchor[click]{unmapfake}
  {Unmap the {\ActiveDVI} logo fake window}
\item \advianchor[click]{closefake}
  {Close the {\ActiveDVI} logo fake window}
\item \advianchor[click]{map}
  {Map the {\ActiveDVI} logo window}
\item \advianchor[click]{unmap}
  {Unmap the {\ActiveDVI} logo window}
\end{itemize}
